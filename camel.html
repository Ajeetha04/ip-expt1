camel

FEATURES

  • Camels are mammals with long legs, a big-lipped snout and a humped back
  • There are two types of camels: dromedary camels, which have one hump, and Bactrian camels, which have two humps
  • Camel has long eyelashes that protect them from dust
  • Large hump that stores water and food in harsh desert conditions
  • A fully-grown camel can weigh up to 700kg/ 1542lbs
